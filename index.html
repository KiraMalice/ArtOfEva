EVANGELION

Enter the horrifyingly beautiful world of Evangelion

Born from the mind and screaming of visionary director Hideaki Anno, Neon Gensis Evangelion,
better known as Evangelion, has turned into one of
many staples of 90s anime and since the beginning of the franchise in 1995
all the way towards the last installment of "Shin Evangelion" in 2021,
has been the topic of intense debate, love, fame and infamy,
praise and controversy. This small corner of a vast amount of fan entries
is simply a small homage towards the story that changed my life.
